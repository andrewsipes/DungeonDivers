Controls:

keyboard:

shift: move camera down
space: move camera up

w: move camera forward
s: move camera backwards
a: move camera left
d: move camera right

mouse: 

up: rotate camera up
down: rotate camera up
right: rotate camera right
left: rotate camera left

controller:

left trigger: move camera down
right trigger: move camera up
left thumbstick left: move camera left
left thumbstick right: move camera right
left thumbstick up: move camera up
left thumbstick down: move camera down

right thumbstick left: rotate camera left
right thumbstick right: rotate camera right
right thumbstick up: rotate camera up
right thumbstick down: rotate camera down
